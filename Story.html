Our Story

Café Our World began with a simple dream — to create a space where everyone could enjoy great food, good coffee, and even better company. Founded by Anil Golharkar and Gaurav Golharkar from Yavatmal, the café opened its doors on 7th September 2025 with a vision to bring warmth and togetherness to every meal.

What started as a small idea over countless cups of chai soon turned into a cozy café filled with laughter, friendships, and the aroma of freshly brewed coffee. For Anil Golharkar his son Gaurav Golharkar, Café Our World isn’t just a business — it’s a reflection of their belief that food tastes best when shared with love.

Every sip, every bite, and every smile here is a reminder that happiness can be found in the simplest moments — right here, in Our World.
